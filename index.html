About

Just Threading Salon is a full service salon. After running Beauty Salon in Nepal for 5 years, we are open in Euless, TX with same good quality of service. While eyebrows and facial threading is our main focus, we also provide services like Henna tattoo, facial cleansing care and hair massage. On Appointment, we analyze your facial structure to give you the best possible outcome in terms of the shape of your eye brows or you can choose which shape you want.

Come and experience the gentle and skillful touch and get the best shape as you liked!
